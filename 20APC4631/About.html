ABOUT US

Our Journey

At Slice of Heaven Pizza, our journey began with a simple idea - to craft the most delicious, mouthwatering pizzas that would leave our customers craving for more...

The Art of Pizza Making

Pizza-making is an art form to us, and every pie we create is a masterpiece. Our skilled pizzaiolos hand-stretch the finest dough, using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients. From our homemade tomato sauce to the carefully selected toppings, we spare no effort to ensure that each bite is a taste of perfection.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that exceptional pizza starts with exceptional ingredients. That's why we partner with local farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to freshness and sustainability. When you enjoy our pizza, you're not just savoring a meal; you're supporting local businesses and the environment

Community and Connection

Beyond serving delicious food, Slice of Heaven Pizza is a place where friendships are forged and memories are made. We're more than just a pizza shop; we're a community hub. Our cozy, welcoming ambiance is designed to make you feel right at home, whether you're dining in, taking out, or ordering for delivery.

Our Team

Our dedicated team is like family, and we treat our customers the same way. We're passionate about pizza, and we take pride in ensuring that every pizza that leaves our kitchen is made with love and care. From our chefs to our servers, each member of our team is committed to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience.

Join Us in the Slice of Heaven Experience

We invite you to join us on this culinary journey, where every slice is a slice of heaven. Whether it's a casual weeknight dinner, a special celebration, or a quick bite on the go, we promise to deliver the same high-quality, mouthwatering pizza that has made us a beloved part of this community. Thank you for choosing Slice of Heaven Pizza. We're excited to serve you, and we look forward to sharing our passion for pizza with you and your loved ones.

Contact Us

Have questions, feedback, or just want to say hello? We'd love to hear from you! Visit our Contact Us page for ways to get in touch.

Our Branches

With three convenient locations across Sri Lanka, Slice of Heaven Pizza is never too far away:

  • Galle Fort: Immerse yourself in history while enjoying our delectable pizzas at our Galle Fort branch. The perfect destination for tourists and locals alike.
  • Kandy City Center: Located in the heart of Kandy, our Kandy City Center branch offers a slice of heaven right in the bustling city center.
  • Mount Lavinia: Enjoy the ocean breeze and picturesque views at our Mount Lavinia branch, where the pizza is as spectacular as the scenery.
