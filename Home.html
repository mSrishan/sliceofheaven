Galle Dutch Fort Branch

Galle, Sri Lanka

Since : November 1, 2015 Hashan Wijesiri

Welcome to Slice of Heaven Pizza Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site nestled along the picturesque coastline of Sri Lanka, is a living treasure trove of history and culture. Its cobblestone streets, ancient ramparts, and colonial-era architecture whisper stories of the past. As you wander through this charming enclave, you'll find a tapestry of art, culture, and tradition that spans centuries. Nestled within the heart of Galle Fort, our pizza shop is a tribute to this captivating history. We've carefully crafted a menu that combines the authenticity of Italian pizza with the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality. Our wood-fired pizzas are a symphony of flavors, prepared with the freshest ingredients and a dash of creativity
